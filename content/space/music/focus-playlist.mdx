What I put on when I need to actually think. Mostly instrumental - anything with lyrics competes with the part of my brain that processes language, which is exactly the part I need for writing code or reading papers.

Lo-fi hip hop, ambient electronic, and the occasional classical piano piece. The goal is a consistent low-stimulation background that signals to my brain: it is time to work.
