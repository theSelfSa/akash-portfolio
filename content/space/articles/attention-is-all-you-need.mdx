The paper that changed everything. Reading the original Transformer paper after working with LLMs for a year was a humbling experience - the core idea is remarkably simple, and the elegance of the multi-head attention mechanism makes the complexity of modern LLMs feel almost inevitable in retrospect.

What struck me most: the authors almost casually mention that the model "can be trained significantly faster than architectures based on recurrent or convolutional layers." That understatement launched an entire industry.
